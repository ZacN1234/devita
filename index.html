Our Story — Neapolitan Soul in Manly Since 2013

From a dream in Naples to your favourite table on Darley Road

Simplicity is the key. You don't need many ingredients to make a tasty dish — you need them to be genuine, fresh, and treat them gently.
— Salvatore Leva, Founder

Walk through our door on Darley Road and you'll smell it before you see it — the wood smoke curling from a Neapolitan oven, the char on a Margherita just pulled from 450-degree stone, the quiet sound of dough being stretched by hands that have done it ten thousand times. DeVita is Manly's home of authentic Neapolitan pizza and Italian cuisine, serving Sydney's Northern Beaches since 2013.

From Naples to Manly

When Salvatore Leva dreamed of bringing true Neapolitan pizza to Sydney's Northern Beaches, he didn't cut corners. Our pizza oven was built in Naples and shipped brick by brick to Manly, constructed with ancient "Sorrento stones" from the Amalfi coast. It's the same stone that has fired the greatest pizzas in the world for centuries.

We use an Italian pre-fermentation technique called biga, preparing our dough two full days before it ever meets the flames. With additive-free, all-natural flours, every base comes out lighter, more aromatic, and beautifully digestible. It's the kind of crust you can feel good about eating — and you'll want seconds.

More Than Pizza

We're a trattoria at heart. Beyond our wood-fired pizzas, our kitchen turns out slow-cooked Rigatoni alla Genovese, silky house-made gnocchi, and show-stopping seafood like our Risotto alla Pescatora. Our antipasto board is the perfect way to start — prosciutto di Parma, wagyu bresaola, Italian cheeses, all served with jams and honey.

BYO wine is welcome (just $7 corkage per person), or choose from our curated wine list, craft beers, and cocktails. Whether it's a family feast with our one-metre pizza, a romantic dinner for two, or a big birthday celebration with our set menu — we'll make it feel like home. Manly's been trusting us with their special nights for over thirteen years now, and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Looking for gluten-free pizza in Manly? We offer a homemade gluten-free base on any pizza. Vegan cheese is also available. Whether you need a family-friendly Italian restaurant in Manly, a BYO dinner spot on the Northern Beaches, or authentic Italian takeaway near Manly Beach — DeVita has you covered.
