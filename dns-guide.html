Your domain devita.net.au is currently managed through Cloudflare (set up by your web developer or hosting provider). To connect your new website, we have two simple options.

⚠️ Your Email Will NOT Be Affected

I'll set up all your email records (MX, SPF, DKIM) on the new system before we make any changes. I won't ask you to do anything until everything is tested and ready.

Option A: Release the Domain (Easiest) Recommended

Your domain is currently on someone else's Cloudflare account (likely your web developer or hosting provider). The quickest path is to ask them to remove devita.net.au from their Cloudflare account. This frees the domain so I can add it to mine.

What to ask your web person

Send them a message like this:

"Hi, I'm moving my website for devita.net.au to a new provider. Could you please remove devita.net.au from your Cloudflare account? The domain will stay registered with Crazy Domains — I just need it released from your Cloudflare so my new web person can set it up."

Once they've done that, I take over

I'll add devita.net.au to my Cloudflare, set up all DNS records (including email), and connect your new website. You may need to update nameservers at Crazy Domains (I'll walk you through it — see Option B, Step 2).

Email stays exactly as it is — I'll copy all email records before anything changes.

If you don't know who set up Cloudflare, or can't reach them, we'll go straight to Option B — it works just as well.

Option B: Change Nameservers at Crazy Domains

If you can't reach your current web person, no worries — we just update the nameservers at your domain registrar. Here's how:

1

I set everything up first. I'll add devita.net.au on my Cloudflare account and copy all your existing DNS records — including email — before we change anything.

2

Update nameservers at Crazy Domains. You (or I can help you) log into your registrar:

  • Go to crazydomains.com.au
  • Click My Account and sign in
  • Go to your Domain Manager
  • Click on devita.net.au
  • Click the Name Servers tab
  • Change the nameservers:
Remove (old):
kara.ns.cloudflare.com marek.ns.cloudflare.com
Replace with (new):
[ Zac's nameserver 1 — will be provided ] [ Zac's nameserver 2 — will be provided ]

Click Save Records to confirm.

3

Wait for it to take effect. Usually under an hour, but can take up to 24 hours.

4

I handle everything else — SSL certificate, DNS records, connecting the domain to your new site.

Timing: Changes usually take effect within an hour, but can take up to 24 hours. You might see the old site and new site alternating during this period — that's completely normal.

I'll Handle Everything Else

Once you've done your part, I'll take care of:

  • Setting up your SSL certificate (the padlock icon)
  • Connecting devita.net.au to your new website
  • Making sure www.devita.net.au works too
